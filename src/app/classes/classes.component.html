Why Vedanta Classes?

Vedanta Institute Visakhapatnam has organized study classes in various parts of the city. They are designed to provide each member a methodic course of study backed by scientific and analytical enquiry. Members are encouraged to engage in an interactive discussion of Vedanta Philosophy and its application to life.

The vedanta classes are conducted by S.V.Satyanarayana(disciple of A. Parthasarathy).
