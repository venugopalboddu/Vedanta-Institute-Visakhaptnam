Vedanta Institute Visakhapatnam

It is a non-profit public charitable trust registered in Andhra Pradesh. It was established under the guidance of A. Parthasarathy. Mainly, It is founded for the purpose of harmonizing the physical, emotional, intellectual and spiritual aspects of human beings, without any distinction of colour, race or religion. The trust particularly focuses on the young minds, motivating them to work for an ideal, beyond selfish interests. Above all, lead one to the ultimate goal of Self-Realization.

The trust organizes annual public discourses on Vedanta in order to spread the knowledge of life and living for the upliftment of the society. These discourses are conducted by Swami Parthasarathy, Sushilaji (a senior disciple of A. Parthasarathy). The discourses are attended by large audiences from a cross section of society. The objectives of the trust are met through donations made to it. All donations are exempt from income tax under section 80G of the income tax act.

Sushilaji

Sushilaji is a guest teacher of vedanta in Visakhapatnam. She is a senior disciple of the internationally renowned philosopher Shri A Parthasarathy. An MSc in Biochemistry from Mumbai University, Sushilaji was a research officer for Johnson and Johnson. During the course of her work and exposure to the teachings of Shri A Parthasarathy, she realized that although science had tremendously improved the world in the past century, the quality of life had actually deteriorated. Sushilaji has since spent three decades in the study, research and exposition ofVedanta. She has addressed large public audiences and premier organizations on ancient spiritual texts. Sushilaji is a Trustee of the Vedanta Cultural Foundation that runs the Vedanta Academy, a unique institution of higher learning near Mumbai.


S.V.Satyanarayana

Mr. S.V.Satyanarayana is a local teacher of vedanta in Visakhapatnam. He is an engineering graduate from AndhraUniversity. After a distinguished corporate career in India and UK(abroad)he took up to intense study and research in Vedanta Philosophy under the direct guidance of his spiritual guru A. Parthasarathy. He has spent five years, full-time, from 2001 to 2006, in the Vedanta Academy, founded by the Swamiji, near Lonavla, 108 kms from Mumbai. He is the resource of Vedanta Institute Visakhapatnam a charitable trust dedicated for the development of overall personality of human beings to gain peace and prosperity in their lives. Since 2006 he is residing in Visakhapatnam holding workshops for the corporates on various management topices, personality development sessions for college and school students. He is also conducting public discourses and study classes on Bhagavad Gita and other scriptural texts.
